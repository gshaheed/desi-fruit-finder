How the stock check works

For online vendors, a script checks each store's public product page every 15 minutes and reads its stock signal — so a badge like In stock, Pre-order or Sold out reflects the last automated check, with the time shown on each listing. Hit the ⚡ Check live button on any of those listings to trigger a fresh, on-the-spot check instead of waiting for the next scheduled run.

Grocery stores don't publish live inventory, so those are marked Check directly. For those, the site also computes a typically in/out of season signal from each fruit's harvest calendar and today's date — so you get an automatic read without visiting the store, though it's an estimate, not a live count. Every listing still has a direct link so you can verify before you go.

Listings with a real price and an in stock or pre-order badge can be added to your cart and purchased on site at the vendor price plus a 5% brokerage fee — see Order on site for details.

Buy on site — how it works

When a vendor listing shows a real price and is in stock or pre-order, you can add it to your cart and pay here. We charge the vendor's listed price plus a 5% brokerage fee for placing and coordinating the order.

  • You pay once through Stripe (card, Apple Pay, Google Pay where available).
  • We broker the sale — after payment we order from the vendor at their listed price and pass along your shipping details.
  • Fulfillment is by the vendor — shipping times, pickup, and perishable handling follow their policies. Always confirm availability before travel for in-store vendors.
  • No price on the listing? Use Request order to message the vendor yourself — we don't guess prices.

Desi Fruit Finder is an order facilitator, not the merchant of record for vendor inventory. Perishable food sales may be subject to local regulations.

Why this exists

The best desi and tropical fruits show up for only a few weeks a year, and the good stock vanishes fast. This finder was built to answer one question quickly: where can I actually buy this fruit right now, near me?

It covers South Asian favourites and the wider tropical world, from Alphonso mango and jamun to mangosteen, rambutan and dragon fruit — with real vendors across California and nationwide shipping. Vendor coverage keeps growing.
