🥭 Desi Fruit Finder

Regional Indian fruits, tracked before they're gone — Northern & Southern California

Alphonso mangoes, jamun, chikoo, sitaphal — these fruits fly in from India for a few short weeks a year, and stock at Bay Area and LA-area vendors disappears fast. This page tracks known vendors and, where possible, checks their live stock status automatically every day. See how it works.

General seasonal windows for these fruits when imported from India. Actual arrival dates shift year to year — the live status above is the more reliable signal.

How this works

Vendor listings are hand-researched. For online sellers with a public product page, a small script (scraper.py) runs automatically once a day via GitHub Actions, reads the page text, and flags whether it looks in stock, pre-order, or sold out/season-ended — the "Last checked" timestamp on each card shows when that last ran. Vendors marked "check directly" (physical stores, marketplaces) don't have a reliably scrapeable stock signal, so call ahead or check their site.

This is a best-effort heuristic based on public page text, not a guarantee of real-time inventory. Always confirm with the vendor before making a special trip.
