A guide made by hand, not by algorithm.

Most fruit charts stop at apples and bananas. This one starts where those leave off — with the fruits people actually grow up on across the subcontinent and the wider tropics, the ones sold from handcarts and eaten over a sink because they're too juicy for a plate.

Every fruit here is drawn from scratch, described plainly, and tagged so you can find it by taste or season. No stock photos, no filler.
